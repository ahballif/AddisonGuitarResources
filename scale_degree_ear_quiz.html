Scale Degree Ear Quiz

The following exercise is designed to help train you to hear notes based on their relation to the root of the key. Upon pressing the "Play Sound" button you will hear a note. This is the root of the key. A few seconds later you will hear a second note. This is the pitch you are asked to identify. After listening, enter the scale degree (of the major scale) that describes the second note.

For a more advanced exercise, you can add pitches outside the major scale. To do so, press the checkbox labeled "Include chromatic scale degrees."

The quiz will stay in the same key for 20 rounds before changing to a new key.

Ready to listen?

Answer using numbers or chromatic labels like 2, ♭3, ♯4, 5, ♭7.
